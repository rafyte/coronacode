A story for Buddy, by Buddy

Once upon a time,

there was a prince.

And he missed his princess very very much.

And he thought about her all day, every day.



And the princess, in her castle far away, missed her prince very much.

And she thought about him all day, every day.



But alas, they could not be together, for there was a deadly plague outside in the world.





And so they loved each other, from far away.
